Classic Nachos

Learn how to make nachos with our simple and speedy recipe. These cheesy nachos are served topped with tomato salsa and crunchy spring onions. Serve them with refried beans and sour cream for dipping.

Details
Cook time Total time Servings
5 min 5 min 3

Ingredients

  • 175g packet plain tortilla chips
  • 225g jar salsa
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • A few sliced spring onions
  • A good chunk of cheese
  • A pinch of crushed chili flakes
  • Soured cream and/or guacamole (optional)

Directions

  1. Tip tortilla chips onto a baking tray, pour over salsa, and scatter over a few sliced spring onions.
  2. Grate over a good chunk of cheese (fontina is perfect, but cheddar will do). Add a pinch of crushed chili flakes, then put under a hot grill for about 3 minutes to melt the cheese. Serve with soured cream and/or guacamole (optional).
